About

Trending coffees people love right now — and what goes into each cup (ingredients only).

Cold Brew

Ingredients:

  • Coarsely ground coffee
  • Cold water
  • Ice (to serve)
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Simple syrup (optional)

Iced Latte

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Ice
  • Vanilla syrup (optional)

Matcha Espresso Fusion

Ingredients:

  • Matcha powder
  • Water (for whisking)
  • Espresso
  • Milk (optional)
  • Ice (optional)

Spanish Latte

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Milk
  • Ice (optional)
  • Cinnamon (optional)

Affogato

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Vanilla ice cream / gelato
  • Cocoa powder or chocolate shavings (optional)

Mocha

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Cocoa powder or chocolate sauce
  • Milk
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Caramel Macchiato

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Milk
  • Vanilla syrup
  • Caramel sauce

Dirty Chai

Ingredients:

  • Chai concentrate / brewed chai tea
  • Espresso
  • Milk
  • Ice (optional)
  • Cinnamon (optional)
