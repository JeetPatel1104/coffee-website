About

At Brew & Bean, coffee is personal, seasonal, and always crafted with intention.

How we think about coffee

We design every menu with balance in mind: bold coffees for quick energy, smooth milk-based options for everyday comfort, and a few adventurous drinks for weekends. Our baristas focus on ingredients that bring out flavor naturally rather than masking it.

What people are drinking right now

Cold Brew is trending for its clean finish and simple base of coarse coffee, cold water, and ice, while Iced Lattes keep things creamy with espresso, milk, and optional vanilla. For richer cups, Mocha blends espresso, milk, and chocolate, and Caramel Macchiato layers espresso, milk, vanilla syrup, and caramel.

Signature combinations

We also serve combinations that feel unique but familiar: Spanish Latte uses espresso with condensed milk and regular milk, Dirty Chai combines chai, espresso, and milk, and Affogato pairs espresso with vanilla ice cream for a dessert-style coffee moment.

Our ingredient promise

Whether it is a classic espresso or a custom builder drink, we stay focused on quality ingredients: fresh-roasted coffee, clean water, milk options, and thoughtful flavor add-ons like vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, and chocolate.
